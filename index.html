Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science student at Tel Aviv University (BSc, minor in Entrepreneurship) with a deep fascination for how computers work at the lowest levels. My coursework spans data structures, computer architecture, and software design — all with strong grades.

Before university I served 3 years in the IDF as a Combat Soldier and Operations Sergeant at the 162 Division fire center during the Swords of Steel war. That experience gave me discipline, leadership under pressure, and the ability to perform in high-stakes environments.

My ambition is to work close to the metal: Linux kernel development, OS internals, and systems tools. In my spare time I'm working on a Game Boy emulator in C — engineering the CPU fetch-decode-execute cycle to deeply understand hardware-software interfacing at the lowest level.
